Pumpkin Pie

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 can pumpkin puree
  • 1 can sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 2 egg whites

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F.
  2. Mix all the ingredients in a large bowl.
  3. Pour everything into a pie shell.
  4. Bake for 14 minutes.
  5. Reduce the heat to 350 degrees and bake for an additional 40 minutes.
