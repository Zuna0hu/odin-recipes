Pumpkin Turkey Chili

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped green bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup chopped yellow bell pepper
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 2 cups pumpkin puree

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add onion, green bell pepper, yellow bell pepper and garlic, cook and stir until tender.
  3. Stir in turkey and cook until evenly brown; drain.
  4. Mix in tomatoes and pumpkin.
  5. Season with chili powder, pepper and salt.
  6. Reduce heat to low and simmer 15 to 20 minutes.
  7. Serve topped with Cheddar cheese and sour cream.
