Bibimbap

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 cucumber
  • 1/4 cup gochujang
  • 1 bunch fresh spinach
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 carrots

Steps

  1. Stir together cucumber pieces and gochujang paste in a bowl.
  2. Add 2 cups of water to boil in a skillet and stir in spinach.
  3. Drain spinah and set aside.
  4. Stir in garlic and cook until fragrant and set aside.
  5. Cook brown beef for about 5 minutes and set aside.
  6. Mix everything together with rice.
